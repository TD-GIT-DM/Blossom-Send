About Blossom & Root

A love letter to the season, the soil, and the people who grow it.

Est. 2023

A Kitchen Built on Roots

Blossom and Root Kitchen opened its doors on Hartz Avenue in 2023 with a simple idea: that plant-based cooking, done thoughtfully, could stand on its own — no caveats, no compromises, no comparisons required.

We set out to build a room where a lifelong vegan and a skeptical omnivore could share the same table and leave equally delighted. The kitchen is led by chefs who cut their teeth in Bay Area fine dining, but the feeling here is warmer — closer to a Sunday dinner at a generous friend's house.

Every morning we receive produce from growers we know personally, most of them within a hundred miles of our door. What arrives shapes what we cook. The menu rewrites itself with the seasons.

What Guides Us

Our Values

Seasonality First

If it isn't at its peak, it isn't on the plate. Our menu is rewritten continuously — because a June tomato and a November tomato are different animals entirely.

Farmer Relationships

We buy directly from small, regenerative farms across Northern California. When you ask where something came from, we can tell you the grower's first name.

Hospitality, Always

Warm rooms, generous pours, patient servers. A restaurant is only as good as the way it makes you feel — and we take that seriously.

Zero Waste Kitchen

Scraps become stocks, stems become pestos, peels become powders. What we can't use, we compost. It's cooking the way it was always meant to be.

Allergy-Aware

Fully plant-based, clearly GF-marked, and trained on every ingredient. Tell us what you need and we'll take care of you.

A Neighborhood Place

Dog-friendly patios, a bicycle rack for riders off the Iron Horse Trail, and regulars we greet by name. Danville is home.

The Room

Where We Gather

Warm woods, soft light, linen napkins, and windows open to Hartz Avenue. A patio under the trees. A few small tables, a long community table, and a bar that's just as happy to serve a full dinner as it is an espresso.

Private Events

Rehearsal dinners, milestone birthdays, brand launches — we host buyouts and semi-private dinners for 12 to 60.
